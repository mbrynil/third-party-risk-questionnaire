About Templates

Templates are reusable questionnaire blueprints that save you time when creating similar assessments.

  • Templates include all questions, weights, expected answers, and conditional rules
  • Creating a questionnaire from a template gives you a fresh copy to customize
  • Modifying a questionnaire does not affect the original template
  • Vendors cannot see or access templates directly
